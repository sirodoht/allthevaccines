About allthevaccines.org

This website is an attempt to centralise information on all human vaccines.

Data is sourced from websites linked on each vaccine page. We track most actively manufactured vaccines from the western world. Much effort has been made to verify all info presented but zero guarantees are made.

Similar vaccine list data can be found at:

  • Wikipedia: List of human vaccines
  • WHO: Prequalified vaccines
  • FDA: Vaccines Licensed for Use in the United States
  • NHS: Vaccinations
  • Ontario's Routine Immunization Schedule
